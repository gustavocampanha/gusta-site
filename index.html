About
Gustavo Campanha
Programmer and back-end developer with 3 years of experience. Currently student of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at FGV-Rio...
Approved and classified in Colégio Pedro II and Colégio Militar in 2012, elementary school competition, had his studies concluded at the CMRJ. During this journey, he participated in numerous math Olympiads, having as main achievements Gold in the OBMEP (Brazilian Public School Mathematics Olympiad) and Honorable Mention at OBM (Brazilian Mathematical Olympiad).
Technology lover, thanks to his fluent English, he learned to program by himself at the age of 18. currently program in Python, VBA, C and JavaScript. It also develops in HTML and CSS. Nowadays he has as a hobby studying about the information security.
Because his father always warned him about the importance of personal finances, he developed a taste for reading books in this area and thus managed to develop an interesting knowledge about the area. with the help of technology, he controls his finances almost automatically and is on the path to financial independence.
Continues enjoying innovative challenges, with the main objective of adding, with the knowledge obtained, to colleagues, developing projects or sharing experiences.
Passionate about traveling, he already collects 12 countries stamped, the main ones being: United States, Spain, Canada, Italy, Mexico! Satisfied yes, never tired... can't wait to go to the next destination to meet different cultures and meet new people.
Last but not least, he dedicates part of his time to social actions, participating in a small group that collects food and toys for children with cancer at the Casa de Apoio à Criança com Câncer São Vicente de Paulo, a place close to his country where he was raised.
